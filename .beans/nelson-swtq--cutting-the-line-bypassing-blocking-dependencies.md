Named after Nelson's Trafalgar tactic. When a blocking task is low priority but holds up a high-value dependent, the captain requests permission to cut the line: proceed in parallel, accept explicit rework risk, require admiral approval, red-cell reviews the risk assessment. Source: HMS Agamemnon (Terminology). Effort: Low. Impact: Medium.
